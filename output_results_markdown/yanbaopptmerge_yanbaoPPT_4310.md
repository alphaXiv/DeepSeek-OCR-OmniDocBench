一个铅锤高 6cm，底面半径 4cm，这个铅锤的体积是多少立方厘米？

$$\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{3} \times 3.14 \times 4^2 \times 6 \\ = 3.14 \times 16 \times 2 \\ = 100.48 \text{cm}^3\end{aligned}$$

![Illustration of a boy pointing to a thought bubble containing the text: 先求铅锤的底面积用 <math>3.14 \times 4^2</math>。 (First find the base area of the plumb bob using <math>3.14 \times 4^2</math>.)]()![](_page_0_Picture_4.jpeg)

![Illustration of a conical plumb bob (lead weight).]()![](_page_0_Picture_5.jpeg)

答：这个铅锤的体积是 100.48 立方厘米。
