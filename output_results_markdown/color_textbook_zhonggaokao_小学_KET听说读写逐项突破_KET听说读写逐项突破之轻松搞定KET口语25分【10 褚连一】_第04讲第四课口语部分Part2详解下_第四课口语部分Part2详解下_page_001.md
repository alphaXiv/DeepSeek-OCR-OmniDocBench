### KET 口语 part 2 考试技巧

- Give clear answers
- Only use the information in the booklet
- Can't get higher marks for adding extra information

### KET 口语 part 2 重点句型

- 问名字: What's the name of the show?
- 问日期: When's the show?
- 问时间: What time does the show start?
- 问地址: Where's the show?
