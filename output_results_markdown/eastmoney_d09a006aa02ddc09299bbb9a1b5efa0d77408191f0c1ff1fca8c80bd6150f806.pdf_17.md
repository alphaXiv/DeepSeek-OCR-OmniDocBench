全球PCB产值规模（亿美元）

Bar chart showing Global PCB Value Scale (in billions of USD) from 2007 to 2024E (left Y-axis, 0 to 900). The orange bars represent the scale. The blue line represents the year-over-year growth rate (right Y-axis, -20.00% to 30.00%). The PCB value scale shows significant fluctuation, peaking around 2011 and reaching high levels again in 2022E, 2023E, and 2024E (around 800 billion USD).
