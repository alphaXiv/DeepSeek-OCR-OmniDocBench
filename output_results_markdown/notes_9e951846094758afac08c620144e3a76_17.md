![Diagram of parallelogram ABCD. Point F is the midpoint of BC. Line segment AD is extended to point E such that DE:AD = 1:3. Line segment EF intersects DC at point G.]()![](_page_0_Picture_5.jpeg)

解:  $\because$  四边形 $ABCD$ 为平行四边形  
 $\therefore DE \parallel FC$ ,  $AD = BC$   
 $\because DE:AD = 1:3 \quad \therefore DE:BC = 1:3$   
又 $\because F$ 为 $BC$ 中点  $\therefore DE:FC = 2:3$   
又 $\because DE \parallel FC \quad \therefore \triangle DEG \sim \triangle CFG$   
 $\therefore \frac{DE}{FC} = \frac{2}{3}$ 为相似比  
 $\therefore \frac{S_{\triangle DEG}}{S_{\triangle CFG}} = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^2 = \frac{4}{9}$
